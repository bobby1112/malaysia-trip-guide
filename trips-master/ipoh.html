Discover Ipoh

Ipoh, the capital city of Perak, Malaysia, is renowned for its blend of colonial charm and modern vibes. Once a booming tin mining town, it now stands as a cultural hub filled with stunning architecture, vibrant street art, and mouthwatering cuisine.

Our Ipoh's Story

Once a quiet village, Ipoh transformed into one of the wealthiest towns in Malaysia during the tin mining boom. The citys rich heritage, British colonial architecture, and natural attractions have made it a must-visit destination for both locals and international travelers.

Today, Ipoh balances nostalgia and modernity, offering unforgettable culinary experiences and breathtaking historical sites. Whether you're wandering through its mural-filled streets or sipping white coffee at a kopitiam, Ipoh invites you to slow down and savor life.
